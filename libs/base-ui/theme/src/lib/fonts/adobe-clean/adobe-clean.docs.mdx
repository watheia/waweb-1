Provides individual font faces for the `Adobe Clean` font family.

### Component usage

Font faces are loaded on demand. Each font class needs to be added somewhere
in the entry point in order to use a specific variant. Use the `Adobe Clean`
font family, or any of the exported class names to style text blocks

```jsx live
<div>
  <AdobeCleanLight />
  <AdobeCleanLightIt />
  <AdobeCleanSemiLight />
  <AdobeCleanSemiLightIt />
  <AdobeClean />
  <AdobeCleanIt />
  <AdobeCleanBold />
  <AdobeCleanBoldIt />
  <AdobeCleanExtraBold />
  <AdobeCleanExtraBoldIt />
  <AdobeCleanBlack />
  <AdobeCleanBlackIt />
  <div style={{ fontFamily: 'Adobe Clean' }}>
    <p>
      <strong>abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz</strong>
      <br />
      <em>ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ</em>
      <br />
      <span style={{ fontWeight: 300 }}>
        0123456789.:,;()*!?'@#&lt;&gt;$%&amp;^+-=~
      </span>
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.light}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.lightIt}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.semiLight}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.semiLightIt}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.it}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.bold}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.boldIt}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.extraBold}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.extraBoldIt}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.black}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
    <p className={adobeClean.blackIt}>
      The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
```
